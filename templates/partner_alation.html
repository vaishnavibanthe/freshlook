Data Cataloging and Discovery

Alation’s robust data cataloging feature enables organizations to organize and classify their data assets. It simplifies the process of data discovery, making it easier for users to find the information they need. Alation's intuitive search interface provides quick access to relevant datasets.

Data Governance and Compliance

Alation streamlines the process of ensuring compliance with industry standards and regulations. It allows organizations to enforce data governance policies, such as data access controls, privacy regulations, and audit tracking, helping to mitigate risks.

Collaboration and Knowledge Sharing

Alation fosters collaboration by providing a centralized platform for data stakeholders to share knowledge. Users can annotate datasets, add comments, and engage in discussions, making it easier for teams to collaborate on projects.

Automated Data Lineage

Alation’s automated data lineage feature allows users to trace the origins of data and understand how it flows across systems. This transparency helps organizations ensure data quality, reduce errors, and improve accountability.

Stewardship and Quality

Alation empowers data stewards with the tools they need to monitor and manage data quality. It helps identify and resolve data inconsistencies, redundancies, and errors, ensuring that your data is accurate, up-to-date, and trustworthy.
