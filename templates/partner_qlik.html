Qlik Cloud Data Integration

Qlik Replicate

Artha Solutions accelerates data replication, ingestion, and streaming across databases and data platforms with Qlik Replicate. This advanced tool automates replication, eliminating complex ETL coding and specialized expertise, ensuring efficiency and scalability.

Qlik Compose

Qlik Compose automates building, managing, and updating data warehouses and lakes, delivering reliable data to big data platforms. Artha Solutions streamlines data environments by automating multi-source ingestion, creating agile data warehouses, and efficiently managing data lakes with speed and precision.

Qlik Enterprise Manager provides a central hub to configure, execute, and monitor Qlik Replicate and Compose tasks. Artha Solutions streamlines multi-environment management with unified interface for task design, endpoint creation, and real-time replication monitoring.

Artha Solutions helps you leverage its capabilities to make predictions, test scenarios, and drive smarter decisions with AI-driven insights, maximizing efficiency.
