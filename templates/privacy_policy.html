Artha Solutions ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through compliance with this Privacy Policy. We collect several types of information from and about users of our website, including:

We have implemented measures designed to secure your personal information from accidental loss and from unauthorized access, use, alteration, and disclosure. To ask questions or comment about this privacy policy and our privacy practices, contact us at our office locations or submit an inquiry through our Contact Us page.
